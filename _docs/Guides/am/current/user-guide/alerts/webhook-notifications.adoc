= Webhook

You can notify and alert administrators using a webhook.

== Create a webhook notification channel

. In AM Console, click *Settings > Alerts > Notifiers*.
. Click the plus icon image:icons/plus-icon.png[role="icon"].
. Select *Webhook* as your notifier type and click *Next*.
. Give your notifier a name.
. Configure the settings.
. Click *Create*.

NOTE: You can customize the `request body` input text with notification properties.
